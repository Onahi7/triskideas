## ğŸ†˜ Troubleshooting

### "Changes aren't showing"
- Make sure you clicked **"Save Changes"**
- Hard refresh the page: **Ctrl+F5** (Windows) or **Cmd+Shift+R** (Mac)
- Clear your browser cache

### "I can't find the Edit button"
- Look for the **floating pencil icon** at bottom-right
- OR check the **top-right toolbar**

### "Undo isn't working"
- Undo only works for changes in the current editing session
- If you saved and closed, those changes are permanent

### "Section won't delete"
- You might be trying to delete the last section
- Pages need at least one section
