# Quick Start: Changing Your Website Colors

## ✨ What Will Change When You Update Colors?

When you change the colors in the admin panel, these elements will automatically update:
- ✅ **Header** - Navigation links and mobile menu
- ✅ **Footer** - Background, links, and subscribe button  
- ✅ **All Buttons** - Submit buttons, action buttons throughout the site
- ✅ **Page Headings** - Main titles and section headers
- ✅ **Links** - Text links and navigation elements
- ✅ **Icons** - Decorative icons and indicators
- ✅ **Borders** - Section dividers and card borders
- ✅ **Backgrounds** - Section backgrounds and highlights

## Step-by-Step Instructions

### Step 1: Login to Admin Panel
1. Go to your website and add `/admin/login` to the URL
   - Example: `https://yoursite.com/admin/login`
2. Enter your admin username and password
3. Click "Login"

### Step 2: Navigate to Settings
1. Once logged in, look at the left sidebar
2. Click on **"Settings"**
3. Scroll down until you see the **"Color Theme"** section with a palette icon

### Step 3: Choose Your Colors
You'll see 4 color options with color pickers:

#### **Primary Color** (Main color for buttons and links)
- Click the colored square to open the color picker
- OR type a color code like `#ff5733` in the text box
- This color appears on buttons and clickable elements

#### **Primary Dark Color** (For headings and important text)
- Choose a darker shade of your primary color
- This makes text easy to read

#### **Accent Color** (Decorative highlights)
- Pick a lighter, complementary color
- Used for backgrounds and subtle highlights

#### **Background Color** (Light sections)
- Choose a very light color
- Used for section backgrounds

### Step 4: Preview Your Colors
- Look at the preview box that says "Preview Your Colors"
- This shows how your colors look together
- Click the sample button to see the button style

### Step 5: Save Your Changes
1. Scroll to the bottom of the page
2. Click the green **"Save Settings"** button
3. Wait for the success message
4. Your entire website now uses your new colors!

### Step 6: View Your Website
1. Open your website in a new tab
2. You should see all your new colors applied
3. If colors don't show, press `Ctrl + F5` (Windows) or `Cmd + Shift + R` (Mac) to refresh

## Reset to Original Colors
If you want to go back to the default amber/orange colors:

1. Click **"Reset to Default Colors"** button in the Color Theme section
2. Then click **"Save Settings"**
3. Done! Your original colors are back

## Pro Tips:

✅ **Keep it simple**: Choose colors from the same family (all blues, all greens, etc.)

✅ **Test readability**: Make sure text is easy to read on backgrounds

✅ **Mobile check**: View your site on your phone after changing colors

✅ **Save often**: Click save after making changes you like

✅ **Take notes**: Write down color codes you like for future reference

## Using a Color Picker Tool
If you want to explore more colors, use free tools like:
- Google "color picker" (built-in tool)
- [Coolors.co](https://coolors.co) - Generate color palettes
- [Adobe Color](https://color.adobe.com) - Professional color wheels

Copy the hex code (like `#ff5733`) from these tools and paste it into your admin panel!
